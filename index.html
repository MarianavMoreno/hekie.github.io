About us

Hekie is a company that offers delicious healthy cookies made with natural ingredients suitable for people with type 1 diabetes. These cookies are made from different molecular cooking techniques.

  • Mission

    At Hekie, our mission is to improve the lives of people with type 1 diabetes through healthy and delicious cookies made with molecular cooking techniques. We are passionate about creating products that are not only diabetic-friendly, but also nutritious, satisfying, and affordable.

  • Vision

    To be the leading company in the market for healthy cookies for people with type 1 diabetes. We aspire to revolutionize the way people with diabetes experience food, providing them with delicious and nutritious options that do not compromise their health.

  • Values

    Innovation: We are constantly looking for new and better ways to create healthy and delicious products for people with diabetes. Excellence: We are committed to the highest quality in everything we do, from the selection of ingredients to the making of our cookies. Commitment: We are dedicated to improving the lives of people with type 1 diabetes. Responsibility: We are aware of the impact that our products have on the health and well-being of our customers.

Presentaciones

Esferificación

Se trata de emplear uno de los principios de la gelificación, mediante un gelificante llamado alginato sódico, este se une a una sal de calcio para encapsular un alimento líquido.

Aires y espumas

Las espumas, vistas desde la ciencia, son una dispersión coloidal en la que el medio de dispersión es un líquido, y la fase dispersa, un gas.

Liofilización

La liofilización es un método de conservación de alimentos en el que confluyen distintos procesos, como la congelación, el vacío y la sublimación.

Gelificación

La técnica de gelificación consiste en dar una textura gel a un líquido o un semisólido, pero este gelificante antes debe hidratarse correctamente en el alimento que queremos dar la textura.
